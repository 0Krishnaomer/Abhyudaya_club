About the Club

A community built on initiative

Abhyudaya — meaning "rise" or "progress" — began as a small group of students who wanted a space to work on real projects outside the classroom. Today, it has grown into one of the college's most active student bodies, bringing together people from every branch and year.

We run workshops, manage events, build projects, and represent the college at external competitions. More importantly, we're a place where first-year students get mentorship, and senior members get the chance to lead.

Every member is part of a domain — a focused team where you contribute, learn, and grow alongside people who share your interests.
